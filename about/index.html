About Me

I am Yang Ruizhe, a second-year master’s student of Dalian University of Technology.

I am interested in MDE, MBSE domain modelling languages and engineering applications. My current research involves analysing and dealing with the management of heterogeneous system models in safety-critical systems. I endeavour to facilitate the interconnection and automated validation of different MBSE files or models by developing novel software tools and other MDE technology.
